# Statement

Stabilizer code records: each finding is an [[n,k,d]] claim carried with its explicit stabilizer generators, and k and d are re-derived by exact recomputation rather than quoted. The frontier holds the classical small codes ([[4,2,2]], [[5,1,3]], Steane, Shor) as verified certificates and the open [[10,1,4]] existence question as a stated obligation; nothing here asserts more than the certificates re-check.
